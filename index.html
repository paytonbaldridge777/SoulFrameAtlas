Getting Started

A spoiler-light route for your first hours in Soulframe. Think of this as your personal Sparrow in text form.

1. Follow your Sparrow

Your Sparrow is your built-in GPS. Call it when you‚Äôre unsure where to go ‚Äì it will lead you toward the nearest Faction Tale or key point of interest, keeping your progression on-track.

Navigation

2. Defog with World Trees

Seek out World Trees to reveal large portions of the region map and unlock fast landmarks. Grab nearby materials while you‚Äôre there to start building your crafting stockpile.

Exploration

3. Stay in low-level zones early

Focus on areas below enemy level 10 at first. You‚Äôll gain Pact ranks, Virtue points, and weapon levels faster without being one-shot by late-game foes that drop similar loot.

Progression

4. Visit the Blacksmith & Nightfold

As your weapons level, visit Nightfold shelves to unlock Combat Arts and enhance your style. Use the Blacksmith to improve gear, prioritizing survivability (shields/armor) over raw damage.

Upgrades

5. Learn Soul Mode habits

Use Soul Mode to scout camps, mark Omen Beasts, and slip behind enemies for stealth attacks or pickpocket attempts. Treat it like a lens that shows you safe routes and hidden interactions.

Utility

6. Respect grey health

Grey health represents damage you can ‚Äúfix‚Äù before it becomes permanent. Time your blocks, dodges and partial retreats to avoid having grey health chipped into red.

Survival

7. Align Pacts & Virtues with your style

As you rank up, choose Pacts and Virtues that reinforce your intended playstyle: heavy armor brawler, agile duelist, or utility-focused support. Avoid scattering points across everything.

Buildcraft

8. Farm with intent

When you need a resource (e.g. Sproutfolk Fronds or ore types), stick to one or two efficient loops rather than roaming randomly. Our Maps & Tools section links to community-tested routes.

Resources

9. Use Tales as ‚Äúdifficulty knobs‚Äù

Some Tales are more punishing than others. Use easier ones to test new weapons and Virtue setups before committing to harder story beats and Omen Beast encounters.
