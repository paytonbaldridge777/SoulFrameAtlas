How to use this index

The Atlas index is meant for quick reminders: “what was that enemy again?”, “where do I get this ore?”, “is this pact more defensive or aggressive?”, “is this worth crafting now?”. For deep numbers or patch-specific values, follow the links above to the fully-fledged wikis and patch notes.
